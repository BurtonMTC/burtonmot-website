Privacy Policy - Booking System

When you use our online booking service, we collect and process certain personal data to facilitate your appointment requests efficiently. Please be aware of the following regarding how we handle this data:

  • Your booking details, including your contact information, will be securely shared and processed by our trusted third-party garage management system provider, strictly following our instructions.
  • As part of this service, you will receive automated booking confirmation emails (up to a maximum of two per booking) to the email address provided. These emails are solely for confirming and managing your bookings.
  • Once you complete an online booking, this information is securely synced with our Garage Management System. You will receive an email confirmation summarising your booking details at the email address associated with your account.
  • We, along with our third-party provider, only view, use, or process your personal data to facilitate your booking requests and provide customer support. We do not use your personal data for any unrelated purpose or marketing activities.
  • By using this booking service, you consent to receiving automated notification emails necessary for confirming and managing your bookings.

We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring your personal data remains secure at all times.
